QnA

How does the randomization work?

Basically, the order of the members and roles that you wrote earlier will be randomized first. Then, a random member will be chosen and they will be randomly assigned a role that is still available based on the quota; it does this for each members.

Where is it randomized?

Everything happens in your browser, nothing is being sent to a server so no information that you wrote is saved.
